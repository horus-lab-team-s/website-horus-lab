AI generates a lot of talk, but behind the buzz are very concrete, already-accessible use cases. Here are five examples we deploy for our clients.

## 1. Automated customer service

A well-designed **chatbot** can handle a large share of routine requests — opening hours, order tracking, FAQs — 24/7, in French as well as local languages. Your teams then focus on high-value cases.

## 2. Fraud detection

For fintech players, AI models flag suspicious transactions in real time, far faster than a manual review.

## 3. Demand forecasting

In commerce and agriculture, anticipating sales or harvests helps **optimize inventory** and reduce waste.

## 4. Document analysis

AI automatically extracts key information from invoices, contracts or forms — a huge time saver for administrations and large organizations.

## 5. Personalization

Recommending the right product, content or service at the right moment boosts engagement and sales.

> The secret isn't to use "AI" for its own sake, but to solve a specific business problem with the right tool.
